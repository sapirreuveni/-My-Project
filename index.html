I am detail-oriented, organized, and patient, which allows me to carefully analyze systems and ensure high-quality results. For me, QA is not just about finding bugs — it is about creating better, more reliable products that users can trust. My motivation to learn and grow, together with my strong technical background, make me confident in my ability to succeed and contribute in the QA field.
